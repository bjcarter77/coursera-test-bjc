Adventures of Jimmy

Viking

Jimmy's parents came from the North when he was a tiny baby to give him a better life here in Normandy.

Jimmy dreams of sailing mighty ships and conquering lands and raiding villages like the warriors his great grandparents were.

Jimmy is a Viking.

Norman

Celt
